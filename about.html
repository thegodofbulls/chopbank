Our Mission

To help everyday people grow wealth by investing in the food they know, trust, and rely on.

Our Vision

To become the go-to platform in Africa for food-based investments, starting with rice, beans, and garri.

How It Works

  • Choose a Commodity – Pick from rice, beans, or garri.
  • Invest – Buy a stake in real physical goods stored securely.
  • Grow – Watch your investment grow as prices go up.

Why Food?

Food never goes out of demand. Unlike volatile markets, staple foods are always moving — on the streets, in homes, and across markets. We’re turning that everyday demand into smart financial value for you.

Our Story

Chowvest started with a simple idea: What if you could invest in something as essential as food? From a small warehouse in Nigeria, we’ve built a platform where people can grow their wealth through practical, real-world assets.
