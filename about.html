Our Mission

To help everyday people secure their future by investing in staple foods they already consume and trust — rice, beans, and garri. We’re making food both a necessity and an asset class.

Our Vision

To build Africa’s most trusted food-investing platform, empowering millions to save, invest, and protect themselves from rising food costs while building wealth.

The Chowvest Experience

  • Step 1: Choose Your Food — Select rice, beans, or garri from our app.
  • Step 2: Deposit Flexibly — Connect your card or deposit any amount you have. No pressure.
  • Step 3: Watch It Grow — As prices rise, your food asset grows in value. No inflation worries.
  • Step 4: Redeem With Ease — When your balance is enough, get your foodstuff delivered to your door or cash out the value.

Why Food?

Food is life. It never goes out of demand. Every day, markets move with staples like rice and beans, regardless of the economy. While money loses value to inflation, food always holds value. Chowvest lets you turn this everyday necessity into a shield and a source of growth.

Our Story

Chowvest began with a simple but powerful thought: “What if people could save and invest in food directly, instead of only money?” From our small warehouse in Nigeria, we’re building a future where food is more than survival — it’s security, wealth, and opportunity for everyone.
